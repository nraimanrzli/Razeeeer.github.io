About Razer

Razer is the leading global lifestyle brand for gamers. Established in 2005, Razer has created some of the most innovative and game-changing products, including gaming laptops, peripherals, and software. Our mission is to provide the best gaming experience through cutting-edge technology and community engagement.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create the best possible gaming experience for all gamers, whether they’re at home or on the go. We aim to enhance every aspect of the gaming journey—from immersive hardware to powerful software solutions that bring players closer together.

Our Products

We offer a wide range of gaming products, from powerful laptops like the Razer Blade to precision-engineered peripherals like the Razer Kraken headset and Razer DeathAdder mouse. Our products are designed to meet the needs of professional esports athletes, content creators, and gamers of all levels.

Join the Razer Community

Razer is more than just a brand; it's a thriving community of gamers, creators, and esports professionals. By joining us, you become a part of a global network of like-minded individuals who are passionate about gaming and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
